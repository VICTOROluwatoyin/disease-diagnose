About Medical Symptom Analyzer

Important Medical Disclaimer

This application is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

What This Tool Does

The Medical Symptom Analyzer uses advanced AI technology to provide preliminary analysis of visual symptoms from uploaded images. It offers:

  • Visual observation of symptoms
  • Possible medical conditions that might cause similar symptoms
  • Severity assessment (informational only)
  • General recommendations for next steps

What This Tool Does NOT Do

  • Does not provide medical diagnosis
  • Does not replace professional medical consultation
  • Does not prescribe treatments or medications
  • Does not guarantee accuracy of analysis
  • Does not store or share your medical images

When to Seek Immediate Medical Attention

Call emergency services immediately if you experience:

  • Severe chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Signs of stroke (facial drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulties)
  • Severe bleeding or trauma
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Severe allergic reactions
  • Any symptoms that seem life-threatening

When to Consult a Healthcare Professional

You should consult a doctor or healthcare provider for:

  • Any concerning symptoms, regardless of this tool's analysis
  • Persistent or worsening symptoms
  • Symptoms that interfere with daily activities
  • Any questions about your health condition
  • Confirmation of any analysis provided by this tool

How It Works

  1. Image Upload: You upload a photo of your symptoms
  2. AI Analysis: Advanced AI examines the image for visual indicators
  3. Pattern Recognition: The system identifies patterns similar to known medical conditions
  4. Report Generation: A preliminary analysis report is generated
  5. Recommendations: General guidance is provided based on the analysis

Privacy & Security

  • Images are processed temporarily and not stored permanently
  • No personal information is collected or stored
  • All uploads are deleted after analysis
  • No medical records are created or maintained

Limitations

  • AI analysis may not be 100% accurate
  • Some conditions cannot be diagnosed from images alone
  • Results should be interpreted by healthcare professionals
  • Tool is not suitable for emergency situations
  • May not detect all medical conditions

Legal Disclaimer

By using this application, you acknowledge that:

  • This tool is for informational purposes only
  • Results are not medical advice or diagnosis
  • You will consult healthcare professionals for medical concerns
  • You understand the limitations of AI-based analysis
  • The developers are not responsible for medical decisions based on this tool
